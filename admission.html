What Happens Next?

1

Application Review

Your application will be reviewed by our admissions committee. This process typically takes 2-3 weeks from the submission date.

2

Interview Invitation

Shortlisted candidates will receive an invitation for an interview via email and SMS. The interview may be conducted in-person or online.

3

Admission Decision

Following the interview, you will receive an admission decision within 1-2 weeks. Successful applicants will receive an official admission letter.

4

Acceptance & Registration

To secure your place, you'll need to accept the offer and pay the required tuition deposit. Registration details will be included in your admission letter.

5

Orientation & Start of Classes

Attend the orientation program to familiarize yourself with the school facilities, meet faculty members, and prepare for the start of your academic journey.
