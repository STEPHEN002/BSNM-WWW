About Bweyale School of Nursing and Midwifery

Established in 2015, Bweyale School of Nursing and Midwifery (BSNM) is a premier health training institution located along Gulu - Kampala Highway within Bweyale Town council, Kiryandongo District.

Our institution is dedicated to training competent healthcare professionals through comprehensive programs certified by the Uganda Health Professional Authority Board (UHPAB).

Our Vision

To be the leading center of excellence in nursing and midwifery education in Uganda.

Our Mission

To produce competent, compassionate, and ethical healthcare professionals who will transform healthcare delivery in Uganda.

Admissions

Take the first step towards a rewarding healthcare career by applying to our nursing and midwifery programs.

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements:

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes
  • Credits in Biology, Chemistry, and English
  • Pass in Mathematics and Physics

Required Documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Certified copies of academic certificates
  • Copy of National ID or passport
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Medical fitness certificate

Application Deadlines:

January Intake: October 30, 2025
August Intake: May 30, 2025

Application Process

1

Obtain Application Form

Download the application form from our website or collect it from our admissions office.

2

Submit Required Documents

Complete the application form and submit it along with all required documents.

3

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee of UGX 50,000 via mobile money or bank deposit.

4

Attend Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview and aptitude assessment.

5

Admission Decision

Successful candidates will receive admission letters with registration instructions.
